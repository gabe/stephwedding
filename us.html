About Us

Todd and Stephanie first met back in 2006 in Saskatoon, when Stephanie first moved to the city and was a naïve young girl. Life was not ready for them to be together and they parted their ways only to meet again in the summer of 2011. The couple knew immediately that they would spend the rest of their lives together.

Stephanie Boyer The Bride

Stephanie was born in St. Brieux, where she attended school from Kindergarten to Grade 12. Her parents are Paul and Pauline Boyer, and siblings are Gabriel and Alain Boyer.

Stephanie graduated from the University of Lethbridge in Nursing in 2011. Stephanie works at the Royal University Hospital on the the Cardiology ward as a Registered Nurse.

Todd Grabowski The Groom

Todd was born in Saskatoon and has lived there his entire life. Todd’s parents are John and Tanja Grabowski, and siblings are Tanja Sears and Jeri-Lynn Walton.

Todd graduated from the University of Saskatchewan in Mechanical Engineering in 2009. Todd now works for the City of Saskatoon as a project engineer.
